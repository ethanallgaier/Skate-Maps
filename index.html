Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a skate spot?

Tap the + button on the map, drop a pin at the location, and fill in the details.

How do I report a spot?

Long press a spot and tap "Report" to flag it for review.

I found a bug

Email us with your device model and iOS version and we'll get it fixed.
